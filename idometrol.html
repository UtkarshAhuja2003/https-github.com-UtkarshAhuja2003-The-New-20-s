Idometrol Tablet

Idometrol Tablet is a medicine used in the treatment of amebiasis. It helps relief the symptoms and speed up recovery. Idometrol Tablet is best taken after food. This will help minimize side effects. Take it in the exact dose and duration as prescribed by your doctor. Do not take it more or less. Take it regularly every day at the same time to get maximum benefit. Do jot stop taking the medicine until you complete the full dose.

Uses of Idometrol Tablet

Idometrol Tablet is prescribed for:
  • Intestinal amoebiasis

Side effects

Most side effects do not require any medical attention and disappear as your body adjusts to the medicine. Consult your doctor if they persist or if you're worried about them

Common Side effects of IDOMETROL TABLET

  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Neck swelling

Quick Tips

  • Idometrol Tablet is used to give relief from diarrhea, stomach pain associated with amebiasis.
  • Always drink filter water and avoid tap water. In case filter water is not available, boil and cool the water before drinking.
  • Eat home cook food and avoid outside food during the treatment.
  • Thoroughly wash fruits and vegetables before eating. Drink only pasteurized milk and dairy products.
