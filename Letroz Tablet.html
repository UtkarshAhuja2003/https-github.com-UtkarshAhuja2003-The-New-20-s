Letroz Tablet

Letroz Tablet helps treat breast cancer in post-menopausal women. It may be used alone or with other treatments, such as surgery or radiation. It can also be used in patients with advanced breast cancer to stop the tumor from spreading to other parts of the body.

Side effects

Most side effects do not require any medical attention and disappear as your body adjusts to the medicine. Consult your doctor if they persist or if you're worried about them

Common Side effects of Letroz Tablet

  • Fatigue
  • Hypercholesterolemia (high cholesterol)
  • Hot flashes

Interaction with drugs

Taking this medicine with any of the following medicine can modify the effect of either of them and cause some undesirable side effects

  • Estradiol
  • Ethinyl Estradiol
  • Tibolone

Quick Tips

  • You have been prescribed Letroz Tablet for the treatment of breast cancer or infertility due to anovulation.
  • It can be taken with or without food.
  • Do not drive or do anything requiring concentration until you know how Letroz Tablet affects you.
  • Do not take Letroz Tablet if you are pregnant or breastfeeding
