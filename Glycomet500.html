Glycomet 500 SR Tablet

Glycomet 500 SR Tablet is a medicine used to treat type 2 diabetes mellitus. It helps control blood sugar levels and thus prevent serious complications of diabetes. It is also used to treat a menstruation-related disorder known as Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) in women.

General Information

Salt Synonyms

Metformin (500mg)

Manufacturer

USV Ltd

Storage

Store below 30°C

Side effects

Most side effects do not require any medical attention and disappear as your body adjusts to the medicine. Consult your doctor if they persist or if you're worried about them

Common Side effects of GLYCOMET TABLET SR

  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Taste change

Interaction with drugs

Taking this medicine with any of the following medicine can modify the effect of either of them and cause some undesirable side effects

  • Ketotifen
  • Amiloride
  • Beclometasone

Quick Tips

  • You have been prescribed Glycomet 500 SR Tablet to control the blood sugar level and reduce the risk of diabetic complications such as heart attacks.
  • Chances of weight gain and low blood sugar are lesser with this medicine as compared to other diabetes medicines.
  • Hypoglycemia (low blood sugar level) may occur when taken along with other antidiabetic medicines, alcohol or on delaying/skipping a meal. Carry a sugar source with you for immediate relief.
  • Tell your doctor immediately if you experience any deep or rapid breathing, persistent nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain as Glycomet 500 SR
