Crocin Advance Tablet

Crocin Advance Tablet is a medicine used to relieve pain and to reduce fever. It is used to treat many conditions such as headaches, body aches, toothaches, and the common cold. It works by inhibiting the release of certain chemicals that cause pain and fever.

General Information

Contains

Paracetamol (500mg)

Salt Synonyms

Acetaminophen

Manufacturer

GlaxoSmithKline Consumer Healthcare

Storage

Store below 30°C

Uses of Crocin Tablet

Crocin Advance is prescribed for:
  • Pain relief
  • Treatment of Fever

Side effects

Most side effects do not require any medical attention and disappear as your body adjusts to the medicine. Consult your doctor if they persist or if you're worried about them

Common Side effects of Crocin

  • Stomach Pain
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting

Substitutes

Interaction with drugs

Taking this medicine with any of the following medicine can modify the effect of either of them and cause some undesirable side effects

  • Nimesulide
  • Oxyphenbutazone
  • Metamizole

Quick Tips

  • Crocin Advance Tablet should be taken with food or milk to prevent upset stomach.
  • Take it as per the dose and duration prescribed by your doctor. Long-term use may lead to serious complications such as stomach bleeding and kidney problems.
  • Do not take indigestion remedies (antacids) within two hours of taking Crocin Advance Tablet.
  • Inform your doctor if you have liver disease as your dose may need to be adjusted.
